Now describe your UVic program as a community
of practice, which shares not only resources but
also a set of common concerns and investments.

What are some of those resources,
concerns, and investments?

With those resources, concerns, and investments
in mind, identify one assumption the community
makes about how knowledge should be produced.

This assumption is called a "warrant." It may be
implicit or tacit in community practice,
and it likely pertains to the media or
communication habits the community prefers.
